My Europeana is your personal space on Europeana.

Register for My Europeana and you can:

To save a search, add a tag or save an item, simply login to My Europeana and click on the relevant action from the menu on the left of the screen.

To view your saved items, searches and tags, click 'My Europeana' from the menu at the top of the screen and select either the 'saved items', 'saved searches' or 'saved tags' tab.
