Ceisteanna Coitianta

Here are answers to common questions about Europeana.

Sábháil chuig My Europeana:

Cé a dhéanann an cinneadh mar gheall ar na míreanna a chuirtear ar Europeana?
The institution that holds the material - individual libraries, audiovisual collections, archives and museums decides what to digitise, based on several criteria:
  • What are the most beautiful, historic or highly regarded items that they are most keen to share with the world?
  • Cad iad na míreanna is mó taighde a fhéachann úsáideoirí orthu?
  • Are there hidden treasures little-known items that could be enormously attractive to users once digitised?
  • An bhfuil míreanna ró-shobhriste d'úsáideoirí? Is féidir le digitiú cabhrú le hábhar a chaomhnú chun go mbeadh teacht níos fusa ag údáideoirí air.
Nuair atá mír digitithe is féidir í a chur ar Europeana.
An féidir liom na míreanna a fheiceáil i dtoise níos mó?
In many cases, you can see bigger versions of items on Europeana simply by clicking on the thumbnail. The available size of the item is decided by the institutions that provide content to Europeana.
An féidir liom comhaid ar nós r-Leabhair, ceol agus scannáin a íoslódáil ó Europeana?
Ní féidir leat míreanna a íoslódáil díreach ó Europeana.eu ach déanaimid tú a nascadh le bunfhoinse na míre ar shuíomh idirlín an tsoláthróra, mar ar féidir leat an mhír a íoslódáil. Braitheann sé ar an institiúid aonair cé acu an bhfuil nó nach bhfuil mír ar fáil.
Tá mír lipéadaithe go mícheart. Cad ba chóir dom a dhéanamh?
Details of items in Europeana are provided by the institution holding the original content. Email us the details that you believe to be incorrect, and we will pass your information to the content provider.
Cén fáth nach féidir liom teacht ar an ábhar atá á lorg agam?
Tá Europeana ag méadú i gcónaí. Mura féidir leat teacht ar a bhfuil á lorg agat i láthair na huaire, tar ar ais ag tráth eile le do thoil toisc go gcuirtear ábhar leis an mbunachar sonraí go rialta. D'fhéadfadh go bhfuil cúiseanna ann nuair nach liostaítear mír in Europeana:
  • Níor digitíodh an mhír go fóill
  • Ní comhpháirtí Europeana an institiúid a bhfuil an saothar aici
  • Copyright clearance is needed.
Cén fáth nach bfuil gach rud ar Europeana aistrithe go dtí mo theanga?
We work continuously on issues surrounding multilingualism.
With the help of our partner institutions, Europeana's interface is translated into all the official languages of the European Union, and several other languages. Our virtual exhibitions are also available in different languages.

At the same time, we are also working on enabling cross-language searches. This means, for example, that when you search for "treaty", your search results will also include "traite", "trattato", "traktat" etc. Currently we have multilingual support for some search functions, but in the years to come, the application of semantic technologies will further advance multilingual search possibilities.

We have also enabled a translation function on the portal to help you translate item's details into your language. More information about the search and translation functions is available on our Help pages.

Although we are doing our best, there is still a need to have more content and information available in a wider range of languages. However, we have to balance our limited resources with other priorities, including adding more content to Europeana and improving the technical function of the site.

Rannpháirtíocht agus Idirghníomhaíocht

Is duine aonair mé. An féidir liom cur le Europeana?
Europeana welcomes user participation. Currently, we are running a project "The First World War in everyday documents" that is building an online archive with private memorabilia and stories from the period of the Great War ((1914-1918) from Germany. If you have family photographs, letters, diaries, short films, audio recordings, objects or stories, please share them on the project site. Soon this initiative will extend to other countries in Europe.
    You can also contribute to Europeana by
  • joining our Facebook leathanach
  • visiting our blog
  • signing up for our newsletter
  • aiseolas a chur chugainn on your experience with Europeana.
An féidir liom airgead a bhronnadh ar Europeana?
Faoi láthair faigheann Europeana maoiniú ón Aontas Eorpach agus ó na ballstáit. Táimid sásta deiseanna d'urraíocht chorparáideach agus samhlacha maoinithe a phlé. Is féidir teagmháil a dhéanamh linn trínár Foirm Aiseolais le do mholadh agus cuirfimid do theachtaireacht ar aghaidh go dtí an duine cuí.
An úsáideann Europeana na meáin shóisialta?
Tá fáilte romhant sinnn a leanúint ar Twitter agus a bheith i do bhall dár Facebook ngrúpa. Is féidr le proifisiúnaithe oidhreachta cultúrtha a bheith ina mball dár LinkedIn group.
Conas is féidir liom an nuacht is déanaí a fháil ó Europeana?
Cláraigh le bheith ar liosta preasa Europeana Cláraigh do R-Nuacht Eurpoeana le haghaidh scéalta ar ábhar agus ar thionscadal Europeana. Seoltar an nauchtlitir 6 huaire in aghaidh na bliana. Is féir le baill an phreasa clárú ar ár liosta seoltaí chun foghlaim mar gheall ar ghnéithe nua agus clocha míle i bhforbairt Europeana agus mar gheall ar fhoilsiú príomhcháipéisí beartais.

Copyright & Content Re-use

How can I use items on Europeana?
Please consult our Téarmaí úsáide for detailed information on use of content on Europeana.
An féidir leat leagan ardtaifigh nó bunaidh de mhír a sholáthar do Europeana?
Europeana.eu leads users to the cultural institutions digitising Europe's heritage, but it does not hold the original material. Members of the press can contact the Europeana team via our Press Room for advice on rights clearance. Otherwise, please contact the original provider of the item you are interested in. Below its thumbnail image, you can see who owns the object.
Conas is féidir liom lógó Europeana a úsáid?
Is féidir lógó Europeana a úsáid chun críocha tráchtála agus na meán cumarsáide. Tá leaganacha ardtaifigh dár lógo, le clibeanna i roinnnt teangacha, ar fáil inár Press Room.

Sábháil chuig My Europeana
This is your personal space on Europeana. With Sábháil chuig My Europeana, you can save your favourite items and searches, and add tags to items to help separate them into different categories.
Conas is féidir liom mo sheoladh r-phoist, m'ainm úsáideora nó mo phasfhocal a athrú?
There is currently no way to change these items. We are considering adding this functionality as part of future site upgrades.
An féidir liom cuardaigh nó clibeanna atá sábháilte ag daoine eile a fheiceáil?
Coimeádtar sonraí gach úsáideora faoi rún. Is slí é seo chun smacht a choimeád ar na míreanna a bhfuil spéis agatsa iontu. Má theastaíonn uait nithe a roinnt le daoine eile brúigh ar an gcnaipe 'Roinn' chun leathanach a sheoladh tríd an r-phost chuig cara nó chun nasc a chur ar thairseacha éagsúla na meán sóisialta.
