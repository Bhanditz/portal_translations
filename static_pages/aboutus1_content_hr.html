Europeana: razmislite o kulturi

Započnite virtualno putovanje kroz stvari koje možete naći na Europeani.
See the storyline and image credits for the Europeana Launch Video

Europeana enables people to explore the digital resources of Europe's museums, libraries, archives and audio-visual collections. It promotes discovery and networking opportunities in a multilingual space where users can engage, share in and be inspired by the rich diversity of Europe's cultural and scientific heritage.

Ideje i inspiracije može se naći u više od milijuna predmeta na Europeani. Ovi predmeti su:

  • Slike - crteži, mape, fotografije i slike muzejskih predmeta
  • Tekstovi - knjige, novine, pisma, dnevnici i arhivski zapisi
  • Zvukovi - glazba i izgovorena riječ iz cilindara, kazete, DVD-a i radijske emisije
  • Video - filmovi, filmski žurnali i TV emisije

Some items and topics are world famous, like Isaac Newton's book about the Laws of Motion, crteži Leonarda da Vincija, Johannes Vermeer's painting of the Girl With A Pearl Earring ili objekti o Berlin Wall. Drugi su skrivena blaga, koje čekaju da ih otkrijete.

Oko 1500 institutions have contributed to Europeana. Renowned names such as the British Library in London, the Rijksmuseum in Amsterdam and the Louvre in Paris are featured alongside smaller organisations across Europe. Together, their assembled collections allow you to explore Europe's history from ancient times to the modern day.

Whether you find a celebrated work or a lesser known object, Europeana always connects you to the original source of the material so you can be sure of its authenticity.

For more information, you can visit Europeana Professional, our knowledge sharing platform.
