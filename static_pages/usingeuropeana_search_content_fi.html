Yleinen haku

Searching the millions of items in Europeana is simple. Just enter your search term and hit Haku.

Erikoishaku

You can narrow your search with the arrow to the right of Search All fields and selecting a category: Titles, Creators, Subjects, Dates/Periods, Places. Then enter your search term and hit Haku.

Alternatively, just ask yourself who, what, where or when you are interested in and type these words into Europeana's search box followed by a colon (:). Then enter your search term and hit Haku.

For specific searches, you could:

  • Use the Tekijä filter or enter who: followed by a name of, for example, an actor, author or architect. Your results will display only items for which the name you entered is listed as the creator. For example, searching for who: Vincent van Gogh rajaa hakutuloksesi van Goghin töihin, jättäen pois valokuvat taiteilijasta tai hänestä ja hänen töistään kertovat dokumentit.
  • Use the Aihe tai Nimeke filter or enter what: followed by a type of item, a subject you are interested in or words from the title of the item you are looking for. Your search results will display only items of the requested type or subject or items whose title includes your search term. For example, searching for what: maps rajaa hakutuloksesi karttoihin, tai what: Art Nouveau näyttää Art Nouveau tyylin mukaisesti tehdyt taideteokset, rakennukset ja monumentit.
  • Use the Place filter or enter where: followed by a name of a town, city or country within Europe or around the world. Your search results will display only items which were created in, published in or depict the place you are searching for. For example, searching for where: Milan will help you differentiate your search when you are looking only for objects created or held in Milan, excluding items on writer Milan Kundera from your search results.
  • Use the Ajankohta filter or enter when: followed by a date (e.g. 1945) or a period (e.g. Roman or Medieval). Your search results will display only items for which the date is listed in the item's date field. For example, searching for when: 1984 will help you differentiate your search when you are looking only for items dated 1984, excluding items that have 1984 in their title or description, like George Orwell's novel.

These search functions also have support for different spellings and languages. For example, when you search for where: Milan, hakutulokseen sisältyvät myös objektit, joissa Milano, Mailand tai Milanas on maantieteellisenä kattavuutena.

Fraasihaku

Fraasihaku on myös mahdollista. Laita vain lainausmerkit (e.g. “art nouveau”) fraasin ympärille, jonka haluat löytää, tai kirjoita AND hakusanojen väliin (esim. art AND nouveau).

Hae ilman tiettyjä sanoja kirjoittamalla NOT isoilla kirjaimilla, ja perään sana, jonka haluat jättää pois (esim. Auguste NOT Renoir)

Another helpful search feature is Automaattitäydennys. Start typing a search term. Europeana will suggest words that may match what you are thinking of. Just select the term you want and hit Haku

Rajaa hakua

 

Tehtyäsi alustavan haun voit karsia hakutulosten määrää linkillä Rajaa hakua functions. These are in the grey pane on the search results screen. You can add more keywords to your search, thereby narrowing the search results. Just type your keyword into the Add more keywords field and hit + Lisää. The keyword is displayed at the top of the pane, under Matches for.

To remove a keyword, hit the X icon next to the relevant keyword in the Osumat haulle list.

Voit myös filter your search results:

  • Mediatyypin mukaan - will display only objects of a selected media type (images, text, video, sound or 3D).
  • Kielen mukaan - will display only objects with descriptions in selected languages, i.e. the language of the institution that provided the object to Europeana.
  • Ajankohdan mukaan - will display only objects with selected dates.
  • Maan mukaan - will display only objects from selected countries, i.e. the country of the institution that provided the object to Europeana.
  • Oikeuksien mukaan – will display objects labelled with the selected copyright label(s). This copyright label indicates how you may reuse the digital object, including the preview.
  • Sisällöntarjoajan mukaan - näyttää objektit valituista instituutioista

Select the filter name, e.g. 'By language' to expand it. Then just use the check boxes for the categories you wish the search results to include. The results will refresh automatically. The filter will be added to the Osumat haulle list at the top of the pane.

To remove a filter, hit the X icon next to the item in the Osumat haulle list, or de-select the check box next to the filter category.

You can also select to Sisällytä käyttäjien tuottama sisältö. This means items that have not come from a cultural institution but from an individual user, for example, items contributed to the Europeana 1914-1918 project.

Share your search with friends by choosing Jaa. A full list of social media sharing options is presented. Just select one you want. This also includes print and email options.
